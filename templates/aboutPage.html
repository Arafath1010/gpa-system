About Us

The purpose of this document is to specify the software requirements for the Academic Performance Management and Analysis System for Undergraduate Students (APMAAS). This system aims to provide a comprehensive solution for undergraduate students to monitor and manage their academic performance throughout their university education.
The system addresses the problems faced by students in terms of monitoring their academic progress, calculating their GPA, and receiving timely alerts for repeat/aria subjects. The system also provides information and support for students who are looking to upgrade their class by providing information on the grades required to achieve the desired GPA.
Overall, the purpose of the APMAAS is to empower undergraduate students with the tools and information they need to succeed academically and achieve their academic goals.
